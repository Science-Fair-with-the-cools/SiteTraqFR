What is SiteTraQ?

Every single website on the internet has a carbon footprint. Physical hosting servers use electricity that use non-renewable energy sources to keep our internet alive as we know it. Want to know what a website's annual carbon footprint is? Find out by navigating to the "SiteTraQ" tab. Find out Below!
